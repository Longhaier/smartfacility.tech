# The Secret to Reducing Energy Consumption by 30% in Semiconductor Factories

In semiconductor manufacturing enterprises, air compressor systems are true "electricity tigers." A 200kW air compressor running 24 hours a month consumes over 100,000 yuan in electricity. But rarely does anyone know that 80% of this electrical energy is converted into heat and wasted. Today we discuss how to turn "waste heat" into "treasure."

## How Much Heat Do Compressors Generate?

Taking a 200kW screw compressor as an example:

- Input power: 200kW
- Effective work: about 40kW (20%)
- Heat loss: about 160kW (80%)

This 160kW of heat, if fully recovered, is equivalent to:

- Annual electricity savings of about 500,000 yuan
- Supplying 1,000 people's domestic hot water
- Providing winter heating for the factory

## Three Main Ways to Recover Waste Heat

### 1. Hot Water Recovery

The most common recovery method. Install heat exchangers in the compressor's lubricating oil circuit:

- Water production: 3-5 m³/h
- Water temperature: 60-85°C
- Suitable for: Factory bathrooms, boiler preheating, canteen water

### 2. Air Preheating

Use recovered heat to preheat fresh air entering the factory:

- Can reduce HVAC load by 20-30%
- Especially suitable for northern winter operation
- Investment payback period about 2-3 years

### 3. Generator Drive Type

The most efficient, using waste heat to generate electricity.
