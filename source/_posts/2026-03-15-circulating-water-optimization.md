# Factory Circulating Water System Optimization: Secrets to Halving Your Electricity Bill

## The Overlooked "Electricity Tiger"

Among the many power-consuming equipment in factories, the circulating water system is often a "silent killer."

A certain packaging and testing factory has 3 sets of 132kW cooling tower fans and 4 sets of 55kW water pumps in their circulating water system, running 24 hours at full load. However, actual monitoring revealed: motor efficiency during peak summer load was only 65%, cooling tower filler was severely clogged, and water pump operating efficiency was as low as 50%—wasting over 800,000 yuan in electricity bills annually.

This is the true state of circulating water systems in most factories.

## Core Parameters of Circulating Water Systems

### 1. Temperature Difference Control

**Standard Conditions:**
- Inlet water temperature: 32℃
- Outlet water temperature: 37℃
- Temperature difference: 5℃

Too small temperature difference → insufficient cooling capacity, increased chiller load
Too large temperature difference → excessive fan and pump energy consumption

### 2. Flow Matching

**Calculation Formula:**
> Flow(m³/h) = Cooling Capacity(kW) × 0.172

Excessive flow → increased pump power consumption
Insufficient flow → poor cooling effect

### 3. Energy Efficiency Ratio (EER)

**Definition:**
> EER = Cooling Capacity(kW) ÷ Input Power(kW)

**Industry Standards:**
- Level 1 Energy Efficiency: EER ≥ 5.0
- Level 2 Energy Efficiency: EER ≥ 4.5
- Level 3 Energy Efficiency: EER ≥ 4.0

Below 4.5? Your system needs a health check.

## 4 Immediate Energy-Saving Solutions

### 1. Cooling Tower Fan Variable Frequency Transformation

**Pain Point:** Fans run at full speed regardless of temperature

**Solution:**
- Install frequency converters, automatically adjust fan speed based on inlet/outlet water temperature
- For every 1℃ temperature decrease, fan speed can be reduced by 5-10%

**Energy-Saving Effect:**
| Before | After | Annual Savings |
|--------|-------|----------------|
| 132kW×3 units×24h | Smart frequency control | 150,000-250,000 yuan |

### 2. Water Pump Group Optimization

**Pain Point:** Parallel operation has low efficiency, "large horse pulling small cart"

**Optimization Strategy:**
- Adjust operating units based on load
- Use frequency control to match actual demand
- Regularly clean filters to reduce resistance

**Transformation Case:** A factory changed 4 sets of 55kW water pumps to 2 variable frequency + 2 constant frequency, reducing operating costs by 40%.

### 3. Cooling Tower Filler Cleaning and Replacement

**Problem:** Filler scaling and clogging, cooling efficiency decreased by 20-30%

**Cleaning Cycle:**
- Hard water areas: Once per quarter
- Soft water areas: Once per half year
- Clean immediately upon finding scaling

**Replacement Standard:** Filler used over 5 years or cooling efficiency decreased by over 15%

### 4. Intelligent Control System Upgrade

**Functional Modules:**
- Real-time monitoring of temperature, pressure, flow
- Load prediction and advance adjustment
- Fault warning and automatic protection
- Energy consumption statistics and analysis

**Recommended Brands:** Siemens, Schneider, Honeywell
