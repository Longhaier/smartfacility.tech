# Compressed Air System Energy Saving: The Critical Parameter 90% of Factories Overlook

A semiconductor packaging factory operates 8 units of 132kW screw air compressors 24/7. Investigation revealed: actual air production was only 65% of rated capacity, system leakage rate was as high as 18%, annual electricity waste exceeded 2 million yuan.

## The Overlooked Core Parameter: Specific Power

Specific Power = Motor Power (kW) ÷ Actual Air Production (m³/min)

Lower values mean higher efficiency.

**National Standards:**
- Level 1: ≤ 8.4
- Level 2: ≤ 9.8
- Level 3: ≤ 11.0

## 4 Immediate Energy-Saving Solutions

### 1. Leak Detection and Control
18% leakage means 18m³ evaporates for every 100m³ produced.

### 2. Pressure Classification Optimization
Using separate pressure supply can reduce energy 15-25%.

### 3. Heat Recovery
Screw compressors convert only 15-20% of electrical energy; 80%+ becomes heat.

### 4. VFD Transformation
Comprehensive reduction of 20-35% energy consumption.
