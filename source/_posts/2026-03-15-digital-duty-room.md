# Factory Operations Duty Room Upgrade: Efficiency Leap Brought by Digital Transformation

## Dilemmas of Traditional Duty Rooms

"Phones ringing nonstop, hands busy and confused"—how many factory operations duty rooms is this the real scene?

A certain factory's duty room has 3 telephones, 2 walkie-talkies, 1 handwritten registration book, and various forms stuck all over the wall. At midnight, the phone rings: "Air conditioner in Workshop 3 is broken!" The duty operator flips through contacts, makes calls to maintenance, waits for responses, records... half an hour passes before the whole process is done.

Such scenes play out daily in factories across the country. Where's the problem? It's not that duty operators aren't working hard, but the **system is too primitive**.

## Three Major Pain Points in Factory Operations Management

### 1. Slow Information Transmission

**Traditional Process:**
```
Fault Discovery → Phone Report → Duty Record → Notify Maintenance → On-site Processing → Feedback Result → Case Closure Registration
```
Every step involves "people running around," inefficient, and information easily gets lost.

### 2. Strong Experience Dependence

- Equipment fault diagnosis relies on "old experience"
- Repair solutions rely on "master craftsmen"
- Newcomers are slow to get started; when problems occur, they can only make phone calls

### 3. No Data Accumulation

- Fault records are in paper books
- Repair experience exists in individual heads
- Want to analyze? Can't find the data

## Four Core Modules of Digital Duty Rooms

### 1. Intelligent Repair Request System

**Functions:**
- One-click repair requests, supporting APP/WeChat mini-program/voice call
- Automatic work order dispatching, intelligently assigned based on work type, distance, and availability
- Real-time progress visible, "urging for progress" becomes history

**Process Comparison:**
| Traditional Mode | Digital Mode |
|-----------------|--------------|
| 5-step transfer | 1-click submission |
| 30-minute response | 5-minute acceptance |
| Manual tracking | Automatic reminders |
| Paper records | Full process traceable |

### 2. Digital Inspection System

**Core Functions:**
- Scan/NFC inspection, eliminate "going through the motions"
- Automatic abnormal reporting, no longer "reporting good news only"
- Intelligent inspection route planning

**Technical Support:**
- RFID/NFC tags: Unique ID for each equipment
- Mobile APP: Location + photo + signature
- Backend system: Real-time data upload, abnormal warnings

**Inspection efficiency improved: 40-60%**

### 3. Knowledge Base System

**Content:**
- Equipment files: parameters, manuals, repair handbooks
- Fault cases: historical faults and handling solutions
- Common problems: standardized solutions

**Value:**
- New employee training cycle shortened by 50%
- Fault handling time shortened by 30%
- Experience inheritance no longer relies on "word of mouth"

### 4. Data Analysis Dashboard

**Core Indicators:**
- Equipment fault rate trends
- Maintenance response times
- Spare parts consumption analysis
- Maintenance cost statistics

**Application Scenarios:**
- Find "fault king" equipment, focus on maintenance
- Discover spare parts consumption patterns, optimize inventory
- Evaluate maintenance personnel performance
